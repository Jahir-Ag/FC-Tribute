Satoru Iwata

Celebrating the life and legacy of Nintendo's visionary leader.

Timeline of Achievements

  • 1980: Began working at HAL Laboratory while attending university.
  • 1993: Became president of HAL Laboratory, rescuing the company from financial difficulties.
  • 2000: Joined Nintendo as head of corporate planning.
  • 2002: Appointed as the fourth president of Nintendo, the first outside the Yamauchi family.
  • 2004: Oversaw the launch of the Nintendo DS, revolutionizing handheld gaming.
  • 2006: Introduced the Nintendo Wii, bringing motion-controlled gaming to the masses.
  • 2011: Launched the Nintendo 3DS, pioneering glasses-free 3D gaming.
  • 2015: Passed away, leaving behind a legacy of innovation and passion for gaming.
